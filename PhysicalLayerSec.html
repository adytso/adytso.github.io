Physical Layer Security

Brief Overview

How can information be transmitted reliably to the legitimate user while keeping this information private from the eavesdropper? This is the question that information theoretic physical layer securing is trying to ask. Most of my work in this domain has focused on finding the distributions that optimal secrecy-capacity for a degraded wiretap channel.
